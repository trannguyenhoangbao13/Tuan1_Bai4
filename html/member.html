Membership Options

Diamond Health Club offers a variety of membership programs, including one to meet your specific needs and budget. Pick up a membership form at the club or e-mail Ron Chi to request one.

Individual Memberships

  • Gold: Full access to the club and access to special Gold Member areas.
  • Full: Full access to the club during all open hours.
  • Weekdays: A low-cost option for access to the club from 10 a.m. to 3 p.m., Monday through Friday.
  • Evenings: Access to the club from 5 p.m. to 10 p.m. every day.
  • Early Bird: Discount program for access from 5 a.m. to 8 a.m. daily.
  • Weekends: Enjoy the club on Saturday and Sunday.
  • Seniors: Special membership rates for individuals over 60.

Family Memberships

  • With Child Care: Includes access to the playroom for children ages 6 weeks to 12 years.
  • Without Child Care: A more economical option without playroom access.
  • Couples: Membership for married couples or domestic partners.
  • Seniors: Special rates for senior couples.

Temporary Memberships

  • Monthly: Full club access for one month.
  • Weekly: Full access for a specific week.
  • Daily: Access for one full day, including all facilities.
  • Punch Cards: Pre-paid cards for 5, 10, or 20 visits.

For more information, contact Ron Chi at rchi@dmond-health.com.
